Indefinite Pronouns

someone, anyone, no one, everyone, something, anything, nothing, everything, somewhere, anywhere, nowhere, everywhere

Someone is at the door.
There's nothing in the fridge.
I can't find it anywhere.

Passive Voice - Present Simple

Form: am/is/are + past participle

English is spoken here.
The cars are made in Japan.
Coffee is grown in Brazil.

Passive Voice - Past Simple

Form: was/were + past participle

The book was written in 1950.
The cars were stolen last night.
Was the window broken?
