Direct Speech vs Reported Speech

Direct Speech: Quote exactly what someone said (use quotation marks)

Reported Speech: Report what someone said (no quotation marks, change tense)

Direct: "It's broken."
Reported: The doctor said it was broken.

Tense Changes in Reported Speech

Direct Speech Reported Speech
am/is/are was/were
can could
will would
have to had to

Said vs Told

SAID: No object mentioned

  • S + said + (that) + clause
  • Example: She said she was tired.

TOLD: With object (me/you/him/her/us/them)

  • S + told + object + (that) + clause
  • Example: She told me she was tired.
