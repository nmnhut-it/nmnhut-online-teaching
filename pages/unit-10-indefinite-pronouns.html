Indefinite Pronouns - People

Type Pronoun When to use
Some- someone/somebody Affirmative sentences
Any- anyone/anybody Negative & questions
No- no one/nobody Negative meaning (no 'not')
Every- everyone/everybody All people

Indefinite Pronouns - Things & Places

Things: something, anything, nothing, everything

Places: somewhere, anywhere, nowhere, everywhere

Affirmative: I saw something.
Negative: I didn't see anything. OR I saw nothing.
Question: Did you see anything?
